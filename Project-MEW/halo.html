Halo

About Halo

Halo is a military science fiction media franchise created by Bungie and now managed and developed by 343 Industries, a subsidiary of Xbox Game Studios. The series centers on an interstellar war between humanity and an alliance of aliens known as the Covenant. The Covenant, led by their religious leaders called the Prophets, worship an ancient civilization known as the Forerunners, who perished in a galaxy-wide event known as the Flood. The main protagonist of the series is Master Chief Petty Officer John-117, a supersoldier known as a Spartan, who is often accompanied by his AI companion, Cortana.

Games in the Series

The Halo franchise includes several mainline games, spin-offs, novels, comics, and animated series. The main games in the series are:

  • Halo: Combat Evolved (2001)
  • Halo 2 (2004)
  • Halo 3 (2007)
  • Halo 3: ODST (2009)
  • Halo: Reach (2010)
  • Halo 4 (2012)
  • Halo 5: Guardians (2015)
  • Halo Infinite (2021)

The series has been praised for its engaging storylines, innovative gameplay mechanics, and multiplayer modes. It has also had a significant impact on popular culture and has a dedicated fanbase.

In addition to the games, the Halo franchise has expanded into other media, including novels, comics, and animated series, further exploring the rich lore and universe of Halo.
